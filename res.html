Mediation analysis

Mediation analysis was performed based on the counter-factual framework and the interventional effect (Vansteelandt and Daniel, 2017; Chan and Leung, 2020). The analysis was conducted in R using the intmed package (Chan and Leung, 2020) with 10000 simulations.
Results from the mediation analysis indicated that
  • the indirect effect through GLP_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.919; 0.943% of the total effect was mediated through GLP_diff.
  • the indirect effect through reelin_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.535; 2.330% of the total effect was mediated through reelin_diff.
  • the indirect effect through the dependence between mediators was non-signifcant, p = 0.991.
  • the indirect effect through the interaction between mediators was non-signifcant, p = 0.951.
  • the direct effect was statistically significant, p = 0.000.
  • the total effect was statistically significant, p = 0.000.
