Mediation analysis

Mediation analysis was performed based on the counter-factual framework and the interventional effect (Vansteelandt and Daniel, 2017; Chan and Leung, 2020). The analysis was conducted in R using the intmed package (Chan and Leung, 2020) with 10000 simulations.
Results from the mediation analysis indicated that
  • the indirect effect through GLP_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.209; -15.317% of the total effect was mediated through GLP_diff.
  • the indirect effect through reelin_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.404; -5.093% of the total effect was mediated through reelin_diff.
  • the indirect effect through the dependence between mediators was non-signifcant, p = 0.981.
  • the indirect effect through the interaction between mediators was non-signifcant, p = 0.960.
  • the direct effect was statistically significant, p = 0.047.
  • the total effect was non-signifcant, p = 0.226.
