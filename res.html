Mediation analysis

Mediation analysis was performed based on the counter-factual framework and the interventional effect (Vansteelandt and Daniel, 2017; Chan and Leung, 2020). The analysis was conducted in R using the intmed package (Chan and Leung, 2020) with 1000 simulations.
Results from the mediation analysis indicated that
  • the indirect effect through TNFalpha_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.716; -0.461% of the total effect was mediated through TNFalpha_diff.
  • the indirect effect through GLP_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.814; 2.675% of the total effect was mediated through GLP_diff.
  • the indirect effect through Glu_diff was non-signifcant, p = 0.112; -12.454% of the total effect was mediated through Glu_diff.
  • the direct effect was statistically significant, p = 0.010.
  • the total effect was statistically significant, p = 0.032.
